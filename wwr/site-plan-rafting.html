Overview

Purpose

Our purpose on developing this website is so that it helps our company to generate more revenue. We want people to know more about us as we make our present online. People who will see us online will be able to share with others about us and share our website, thus more people will know about our products and service, that is how we will then generate more revenue.

Audience

The target customers are families who come out to have time out and having fun as a family. We are also targeting schools that brings kids to come experience rafting. Other customers are the travel and tour companies, because they want places where they can take their clients. The plan is to ensure the website is accessible through laptops and mobile devices as this are the main devices people use nowadays.
