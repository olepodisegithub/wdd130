Who is Oaitse: My name is Oaitse Lepodise, born in a village called Diphale (Kweneng District) in Botswana. I was born in 1986. Some of my nicknames that other people might remember are; Setogo, Togo, Mabizela, Leponesa, Matoni, Bizolo.
